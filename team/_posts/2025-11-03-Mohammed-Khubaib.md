[**Mohammed Khubaib**](https://md-khubaib.vercel.app/) is a graduate student at **Florida International University (FIU)**, majoring in **Computer Science**. He is a Graduate Research Assistant in the **Precision Computational Health and Biomedical Data Science Lab (Saeed Lab)** under the supervision of [**Dr. Fahad Saeed**](./1000-01-01-Fahad-Saeed) and [**Dr. Kaoutar Ben Ahmed**](https://www.cis.fiu.edu/faculty-staff/kaoutar-ben-ahmed/).

His research involves developing and fine-tuning deep learning models for **medical imaging analysis**, with a focus on processing **volumetric medical imaging** data to support computational health applications.
