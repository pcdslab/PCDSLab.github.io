[**Avash Palikhe**](https://avashpalikhe.github.io/) is currently a Ph.D. student at the Knight Foundation School of Computing & Information Sciences (KFSCIS) at Florida International University, where he conducts research under the supervision of Dr. Fahad Saeed. He is a Graduate Research Assistant at the Precision Computational Health and Biomedical Data Science Lab (PCDS Lab), also known as the Saeed Lab. His research focuses on Algorithmic Fairness, Health AI, and Trustworthy AI. He has also delivered tutorials at IJCAI’25, CIKM’25, and ICDM’25.
