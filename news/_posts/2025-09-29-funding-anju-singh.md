We are proud to announce that **Anju Singh** has been selected for two prestigious undergraduate research programs:  

- **OURS FIU (Opportunities for Undergraduate Research at FIU)** – providing funding and mentorship to support undergraduate research.  
- **UR2PhD (Undergraduate Research to PhD)** – a national program designed to prepare undergraduates for advanced study and careers in research.  

Both awards come with **funding support**, empowering Anju to continue impactful research at the intersection of computer science, biology, and bioinformatics.  

Congratulations to Anju for this well-deserved recognition!
